CONSTRUCTION SITE SECURITY – SYDNEY MAXPRO SERVICES

Sydney MaxPro Services provides licensed and professional construction site security services. Our teams protect construction sites from theft, vandalism, and unauthorised access, ensuring peace of mind throughout the duration of each project.

Our Construction Security Services

  • Static Security Guards – Constant on-site presence to deter crime and protect assets
  • Mobile Patrols – Regular patrols for larger sites or high-risk areas
  • Access Control – Management of entry points and authorised personnel
  • Asset & Equipment Protection – Safeguarding tools, machinery, and building materials
  • 24/7 Monitoring – Continuous vigilance and rapid response capability

SECURITY SERVICES CAPABILITY – SCHOOL SECURITY (STATIC GUARDING)

Sydney MaxPro provides static school security services focused on student safety, staff protection, and effective site control during school hours and events.

Scope of services includes:

  • Static licensed security officers on site
  • Access control and monitoring of entry and exit points
  • Student, staff, and visitor safety oversight
  • Incident prevention, reporting, and escalation
  • Support during peak periods, drop-off and pick-up times, and school events
  • Full compliance with school policies and WHS requirements

ASSET PROTECTION – WAREHOUSE SECURITY

Sydney MaxPro delivers asset protection and loss-prevention security services for industrial and warehouse environments, ensuring the safety of goods, infrastructure, and daily operations.

Scope of services includes:

  • Static guarding for warehouses and logistics facilities

  • Monitoring of deliveries, dispatch, and loading docks
  • Access control for staff, contractors, and visitors

  • Compliance with WHS, site inductions, and security protocols
